Helix Capital Partners is prepared to commit $1,200,000 over 3 years in support of the MissionGuard AI ethics program. The commitment is subject to the following binding conditions which must be accepted in writing prior to first disbursement.

Binding Conditions

  1. The program shall be re-branded "Helix Ethics Lab" for the full gift term (3 years).
  2. Helix shall appoint one voting seat on the program's advisory board for the gift term.
  3. Recipient shall provide Helix with quarterly access to anonymized donor intake data for internal research use.
  4. Helix retains first right of refusal on co-publishing any whitepapers produced by the program.
⚠️
Reputational & Governance Risk:
Helix is a mid-stage venture firm with portfolio exposure to ed-tech and fintech. No current regulatory actions on file. However, data-sharing and board-seat conditions create significant mission-control and privacy concerns.
